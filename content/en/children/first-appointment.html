First appointment regarding fertility

Ideally, the clarification of the sterility includes both partners from the beginning and starts with a detailed conversation.

So that any examinations you have already had aren’t senselessly repeated, it would be good if you could bring this information and results along.

After a determination of the location we’re going to try to define the additional procedure together. First, this is mostly a completion of the clarification. We will also talk about the question of an optimal preparation for the pregnancy. Among other things it is about the protection given by vaccination, so please, where available, bring the vaccination card.
